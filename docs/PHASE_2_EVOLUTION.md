## ⚖️ The Balanced Scale
*Skill-Based Matchmaking (MMR)*

The gods of war have brought balance to the realm. You will no longer stumble blindly into battle against giants or novices.

- **Trophy-Based Pairing:** Our new **Expanding Search** algorithm finds opponents of similar skill (Trophies).
- **Fair Trials:** The system starts strict, ensuring a fair fight, and only widens its search to ensure you find a match in a timely manner.
- **True Competition:** Every win is more meaningful when earned against a worthy foe.
